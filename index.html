"An irresistible drive to do things, learn things...seek more"

This line appears in one of my favorite travel books and it captures my approach to life: always learning, always improving. This mindset also translates into my professional life where I seek diverse experiences rooted in analytical and interdisciplinary thinking.

I am currently helping teams launch products and developing an improved onboarding experience for future rotationals at Epson. Previously, I worked for local governments, a tech non-profit, and educational institutions--including my alma mater.

I graduated magna cum laude in Mathematics with a minor in Linguistics from CSU, Long Beach. After graduation, I have taken up additional training which has expanded my technical skill set. These include: Code with AnitaB.org’s training in JavaScript & Advanced Python and Correlation One's highly-selective Data Science Fellowship. I also mentor, tutor, and share my story during college and career awareness events.
