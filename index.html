"An irresistible drive to do things, learn things...seek more"

This line appears in one of my favorite travel books and it captures my approach to life: always learning, always improving. This mindset also translates into my professional life where I seek diverse experiences rooted in analytical and interdisciplinary thinking.

Currently, I am working at a multinational electronics company; previously, I worked for local governments, a tech non-profit, and educational institutions--including my alma mater.

I graduated magna cum laude in Mathematics with a minor in Linguistics from CSU, Long Beach. I enjoy sharing the beauty of math by tutoring students in my free time; if you'd like to learn more, please reach out!
